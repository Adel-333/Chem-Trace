Chem Trace utilizes a simplified but highly effective array of sensors to detect hazardous chemical byproducts. By focusing on the MQ-135 (Air Quality/Ammonia) and MQ-2 (Combustible Gas/Hydrogen), we have achieved a high-precision detection system capable of real-time monitoring in volatile environments.
